Job Opportunities and Financial Support

What is JOFS?

JOFS stands for Job Opportunities and Financial Support. JOFS is a service in which you can look for open jobs or financial support.

Why JOFS?

During the pandemic, a lot of people went into financial struggles and/or lost their jobs. In search of recovery, they went to dangerous sites to find jobs. The goal of JOFS is to provide a safe way to offer, find jobs, and financial help.
